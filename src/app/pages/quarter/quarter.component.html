Quarterfinals Summary

The A2SV AI for Impact Hackathon attracted over 1,118 teams from across the continent, bringing together students and mentors from 48 African countries. Participants collaborated on groundbreaking and meaningful projects, supported by 47 mentors from top tech companies including Google, Meta, LinkedIn, TikTok, and Huawei. The quarterfinals just concluded, showcasing innovative solutions and exceptional talent. This event not only fostered creativity and technical skills but also highlighted the potential for AI to drive impactful change across Africa.
